Technology

At ChronoLux, we combine centuries-old watchmaking traditions with cutting-edge technology to create timepieces that are both beautiful and precise.

Swiss Automatic Movement

Our premium watches feature Swiss-made automatic movements, renowned for their precision and reliability. Each movement is assembled by master watchmakers with over 200 components

Sapphire Crystal

All ChronoLux watches feature scratch-resistant sapphire crystal, rated 9 on the Mohs hardness scale. Only diamond can scratch our crystals.

SuperLuminova

Our luminous markers use Swiss SuperLuminova technology, providing bright, long-lasting glow in dark conditions without radioactive materials.

Ceramic Composites

Advanced ceramic materials offer exceptional scratch resistance and lightweight comfort while maintaining a luxurious appearance.

Precision Engineering

Every ChronoLux watch undergoes rigorous testing to ensure accuracy within +/- 4 seconds per day. Our master watchmakers inspect each timepiece through a 47-point quality control process.
